Report: act_report¶

I want to find out what kind of dogs is most favorite. Therefor, I use the dataset to answer 3 questions below:

  • Most common dogs' stages
  • Most common dogs' names
  • Highest dogs' ratings, retweets and favorities count by dogs' stages

Most common dogs' stages¶

Puper has the most tweet, almost triple that number of doggo which places second. Puppo and floofer are in tthe last ranks with inconsiderable numbers.

Most common dogs' names¶

Charlie is the most favorite name for dogs. 11 of the dogs were named Charlie and its the most we have. A close second will be Lucy, Cooper and Oliver, with 10 dogs being named as these.

Highest dogs' ratings, retweets and favorities count by dogs' stages¶

Another interesting point is the huge increase in retweeting and favoriting of tweets having a mention of the "dog stage" (i.e. Doggo, Floofer or Puppo), the exception being Pupper. Pupper, being a small doggo, usually younger, is probably less popular as compared to its older counterparts.
